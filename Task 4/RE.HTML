ROYAL ENFIELD

CLASSIC 350

Built for all roads,Built for no roads

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is not just a motorcycle; it's a piece of history. With a legacy that dates back to the post-war era, the Classic 350 embodies the essence of a bygone age while offering the comforts of modern engineering. Its iconic design, with the teardrop fuel tank, classic fenders, and single-saddle seat, pays homage to its heritage while standing the test of time.

Beneath the retro exterior lies a powerful and reliable engine, finely tuned for today’s roads. The 349cc, air-cooled, single-cylinder engine offers a perfect blend of power and efficiency, making it a joy to ride whether you're cruising through city streets or exploring the open highways.

The thump of the engine, so uniquely Royal Enfield, resonates with every twist of the throttle, making each ride an immersive experience.

While the Classic 350 stays true to its roots, it is equipped with modern features to enhance your riding experience. The updated dual-channel ABS ensures your safety on every ride, while the digital-analog instrument cluster keeps you informed with a touch of nostalgia.

The bike's ergonomic design ensures comfort on long journeys, with a smooth ride that only a Royal Enfield can deliver.

A Lifestyle, Not Just a Motorcycle

Owning a Royal Enfield Classic 350 is about embracing a lifestyle. It's for those who appreciate the finer things in life, who cherish the journey as much as the destination. Whether it's the early morning rides through misty hills or the evening cruises along the coast, the Classic 350 is your perfect companion.

Available Colors and Customization

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 comes in a variety of stunning colors, each telling a different story. From the rugged Stealth Black to the vibrant Chrome Red, there's a shade for every rider. Moreover, with a range of customization options, you can truly make your Classic 350 your own, reflecting your personality and style.
